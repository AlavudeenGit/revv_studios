FAQ

Questions we get asked often.

How is AI video actually used in your production?

AI handles avatars, voiceover, and first-pass edits — our team directs the script, brand tone, and final cut so nothing feels generic or off-brand.

Do you work with clients outside India?

Yes. We currently support clients across the UAE and GCC region alongside our Chennai-based client base, working fully remote with async reviews.

What's the typical turnaround for a website?

Landing pages ship in 1-2 weeks. Full corporate websites or custom web apps typically run 4-8 weeks depending on scope.

Can you manage ads and content together?

Yes — our Scale plan bundles content production with Google and Meta ad management so creative and media buying stay in sync.

Do you sign NDAs and long-term contracts?

We're happy to sign NDAs on request. Engagements start month-to-month, with longer terms available once we've proven fit.
